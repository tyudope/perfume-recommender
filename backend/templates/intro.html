Perfume Recommender
A quick guide to getting great suggestions.

1) Tell us your taste

  • Liked perfumes: add 1–3 you enjoy (e.g., Dior Sauvage, Bleu de Chanel).
  • Preferred notes: e.g., citrus, woody, vanilla.
  • Use-cases: toggle chips like office / date / summer / winter.

2) Refine with filters

  • Price range in PLN.
  • Rating & count: minimum quality and popularity thresholds.
  • Longevity / Sillage: higher = stronger/longer lasting.
  • Gender: Male, Female, or Unisex.

3) (Optional) AI explanations

  • Check Include AI reasoning to see a small expert summary for top results.
  • We cap the number of items explained per request to control latency/cost.

Tips

  • Start broad, then narrow down with filters.
  • Try mixing one liked perfume + 2 notes for best semantic matches.
  • Use the Fragrantica link to deep-dive any pick.
